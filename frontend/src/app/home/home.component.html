The Rustic Batch

Small-batch cookies made with stone-ground flour, organic butter, and a whole lot of heart. Delivered fresh from our hearth to your home.

"Old World methods, New World flavors."

Our Heritage

The Rustic Batch began in a small cabin kitchen with a simple mission: to restore the dignity of the humble cookie. We don't use industrial mixers or artificial extracts. Every batch is folded by hand and baked in a wood-fired hearth.

"We believe the best ingredients are the ones you can recognize. Farm-fresh eggs, locally milled flour, and seasonal berries from the mountain."
